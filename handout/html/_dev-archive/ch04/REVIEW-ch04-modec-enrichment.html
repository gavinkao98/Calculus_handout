§4.4 Rolle & MVT —— +1 caution

4.4-D1 low · borderline　Corollary 4.3 的假逆命題（單向性）

Corollary 4.3 runs in one direction only: a positive derivative forces strict increase, not the reverse. A strictly increasing function need not have a strictly positive derivative everywhere. The function \(f(x) = x^{3}\) is strictly increasing on all of \(\mathbb{R}\), yet \(f'(0) = 0\); the derivative is allowed to touch zero at isolated points without spoiling strict monotonicity. The corollary is a sufficient test, not a characterisation. 〔critic 建議砍除的原結尾自指句：— when we read strict increase off \(f'>0\) below, we are using the safe direction, and the examples are chosen so that direction applies.〕

§4.5 Monotonicity & Logarithm —— +1 caution

4.5-D1 medium · 強留　對數積律的過度推廣陷阱

The product law converts a product inside the logarithm into a sum outside it: \(\ln(ab) = \ln a + \ln b\). It says nothing about a sum inside. In general \(\ln(a + b) \ne \ln a + \ln b\) — take \(a = b = 1\), where the left side is \(\ln 2\) and the right side is \(0\). Likewise \(\ln(a/b)\) is \(\ln a - \ln b\), not \(\ln a / \ln b\). The proof above earns the one identity that holds; it grants no licence to the look-alikes.

why：本節既有 caution 只管「ln 的定義域(x>0)」，未管「ln 的運算陷阱」。剛證完的 Prop 4.3 積律正是學生最易過度推廣處——把積律誤搬到 \(\ln(a+b)\) 或誤寫成 \(\ln a/\ln b\) 是公認對數第一錯誤。含一行可驗反例（a=b=1 → ln2 vs 0），非空泛告誡。未被任何既有 marker 或 ROADMAP pitfall 涵蓋、locus 唯一自然。critic：兩提案中最強、非 padding，留。數學自核：ln2≈0.693≠0 ✓；ln(a/b)=ln a−ln b（≠ ln a/ln b）✓。
