§3.1 Derivatives of the Sine and Cosine Functions

mediumC-1caution
locus：接 Proposition 3.2（\(\lim_{\theta\to0}\sin\theta/\theta=1\)）的 payoff 處，與既有 radian Caution 並列（其前或其後）。
擬補（乾淨版，推薦）：
Proposition 3.2 is a statement about the limit as \(\theta\to0\), not an identity. At a fixed angle the ratio \(\sin\theta/\theta\) is generally not \(1\): at \(\theta=\tfrac{\pi}{2}\) it equals \(\dfrac{\sin(\pi/2)}{\pi/2}=\dfrac{1}{\pi/2}=\dfrac{2}{\pi}\approx0.64\), and at \(\theta=\pi\) it equals \(\dfrac{0}{\pi}=0\). The value \(1\) is reached only in the limit, as \(\theta\) shrinks to \(0\).
變體（加 forward-ref，覆蓋更常見的學生錯）：末尾再加一句——“More subtly, the argument of the sine must be exactly what divides it: a ratio like \(\sin(5x)/x\) does not tend to \(1\) either (it tends to \(5\), as the chain rule of §3.2 will explain).” 略長；\(\sin(5x)/x\) 的「為何是 5」需 §3.2 代換，故只點到、不在此推。
why：守「把 \(\sin\theta/\theta=1\) 誤當恆等式」這個真實常見誤解（檢查表第 4/5 項）。與既有 radian caution 正交（那管度量單位、這管極限 vs 恆等式）。乾淨版用固定角反例、自足不越 D-邊界（不需 chain rule）。數學已核：\(\sin(\pi/2)/(\pi/2)=2/\pi\approx0.637\)、\(\sin\pi/\pi=0\)。
