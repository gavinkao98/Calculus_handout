G1-4.2-1advisory · F4 句長建議採
locus　§4.2 開節（sec-4-2.html 行 40）：一句以巢狀「that…that…and that…」並列三項待證命題，自學讀者一次須扛三 clause＋一層「the way the symbol promises」鋪陳。
diff　What we have not yet shown is that \(e^{x}\) behaves the way the symbol promises: that it is continuous, that it obeys. Three things remain: that it is continuous; that it obeys \(e^{x} e^{y} = e^{x + y}\), and that; and that it even makes sense for \(x \le 0\).
為何　純切句＋分號化三項；先讓讀者 register「行為待證」，再分項列出。承載位（節開場 roadmap 句）的真實可讀性收益。
G1-4.3-1advisory · F1 局部冗餘建議採
locus　§4.3 difference quotient 小節開頭（sec-4-3.html 行 46）：「a single algebraic move, and that move is」在同句內把「move」重述一次。
diff　The entire calculation rests on a single algebraic move, and that move is the exponent law. — the exponent law.
為何　破折號直接揭示，去掉同句重述、零語意損失，且強調手勢更俐落。
G1-4.5-1advisory · F4 句長建議採
locus　§4.5 開節（sec-4-5.html 行 59）：「Less obviously…」一句串 role-claim＋冒號解拆＋「not free gifts」隱喻共四 clause，讀者尚無形式錨點。
diff　Less obviously, it also powers everything we then prove about that inverse:. theThe continuity of \(\ln\) and its differentiability are not free gifts that come with the definition. Each; each needs its own argument, and both arguments reach back to the strict monotonicity of \(e^{x}\).
為何　冒號改句點分段、第二句改分號接續、刪重複「arguments」；building/powering 對比與語意全保留。
G1-4.5-2advisory · F4 句長建議採
locus　§4.5「Defining the natural logarithm」導句（sec-4-5.html 行 64）：一句夾長破折號插入語＋第二 clause，讀者解析三層巢狀才到承載 payoff（每個 \(x>0\) 恰一指數）。
diff　We take this for granted here —: the two limits follow from the series and the intermediate values from continuity, but the details would detain us — and read off the consequence we need:. The consequence we need is what matters — for every \(x > 0\) there is exactly one exponent producing it.
為何　把長插入語斷成獨立句，payoff 另起；「we don't prove this」的誠實訊號保留。
