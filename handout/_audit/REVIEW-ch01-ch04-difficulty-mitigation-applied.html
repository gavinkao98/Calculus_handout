…there is a number \(\delta > 0\) and points \(y_{j} \to x_{0}\) (all in \((0, \infty)\)) with \(\lvert \ln y_{j} - \ln x_{0} \rvert \ge \delta\) for every \(j\). (To see why failure is witnessed this way, negate the limit statement of §1.6: if \(\lim_{x \to x_{0}} \ln x = \ln x_{0}\) fails, there is some tolerance \(\delta > 0\) that no input-closeness requirement can meet — for every \(d > 0\), some point \(y\) with \(0 < \lvert y - x_{0} \rvert < d\) has \(\lvert \ln y - \ln x_{0} \rvert \ge \delta\). Picking one such point \(y_{j}\) for each \(d = \tfrac{1}{j}\) produces the sequence, and for small \(d\) these points stay inside \((0, \infty)\).)

「不連續 ⇒ 存在序列見證」用到未建立的 sequential criterion 否定式，是「全章最真實的『假設了未給的東西』」；模擬讀者只能「信仰式放行」。補的兩行把它化約回讀者已有的 §1.6 ε-δ 否定，並交代 \(d=1/j\) 的取點來源。
