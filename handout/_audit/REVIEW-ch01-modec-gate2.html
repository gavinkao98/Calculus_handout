Issue: The final pronoun in “its outputs” has an avoidably loose antecedent. The intended parallel is clear, but the sentence is explaining domain/range reversal, so naming \(f^{-1}\) again prevents a possible misread.

Original text:

The idea is simply that the inverse trades the roles of input and output.
