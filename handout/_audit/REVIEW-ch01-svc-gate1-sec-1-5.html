建議套用 — G1-2 piecewise 導句講出「為何分情況」

G1-2V1Voice｜Limits of piecewise-defined functions 子節導句（line 188）
Some limits are easiest to analyse by splitting into cases.
為什麼值得套用

中性偏平——只說「分情況最容易」，未說 piecewise 為何逼我們分（左右支不同公式 → 單側極限可能不一致，如 Ex 1.33 的 \([x]\)）。§3 motivation-before-formalism 此處可補。

建議：套用。Some limits are easiest to analyse by splitting into cases. When a function is defined by different formulas on either side of \(a\), the one-sided limits can disagree, so we analyse each branch separately.
