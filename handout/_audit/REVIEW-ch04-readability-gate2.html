原文Also \(\lvert e^{y} \rvert \le \sum_{n=0}^{\infty} \lvert y \rvert^{n}/n! = e^{\lvert y \rvert} \le e^{L}\), and likewise \(\lvert P_{k}(x) \rvert \le e^{L}\).
建議Also \(\lvert e^{y} \rvert \le \sum_{n=0}^{\infty} \lvert y \rvert^{n}/n! = e^{\lvert y \rvert} \le e^{L}\) (term by term, since \(\lvert y \rvert \le L\) gives \(\lvert y \rvert^{n} \le L^{n}\); monotonicity of \(e^{t}\) is not yet available), and likewise \(\lvert P_{k}(x) \rvert \le e^{L}\).
