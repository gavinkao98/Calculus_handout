唯一 finding：2.3-3（second-derivative 記號 caution）— blocking, math/consistency

Codex issue：初稿把 \(dx^{2}\) 解釋為 \((dx)^{2}\)，等於把 \(dx\) reify 成一個可平方的因子——但本書在 §2.2 的 caption（2.2-1，本輪同批寫入）才剛告訴學生「\(dy/dx\) 不是分數、\(dx\) 沒有獨立意義」。兩塊自相矛盾；且與既有 Remark 2.5 的 operator 讀法不一致。屬內部一致性／數學讀法的 blocking。
原文（gate-2 前）
Read the Leibniz symbol for the second derivative with care. In \(\dfrac{d^{2}y}{dx^{2}}\), the bottom \(dx^{2}\) means \((dx)^{2}\) — the operator \(\dfrac{d}{dx}\) applied twice — not \(d(x^{2})\); and the top \(d^{2}y\) means \(d\) applied twice to \(y\), not \(d\) times \(2y\). …
改寫（gate-2 後，採 operator 讀法、不 reify \(dx\)）
Read the second-derivative symbol as a single unit, not as a fraction to pull apart. The whole expression \(\dfrac{d^{2}y}{dx^{2}}\) stands for the operator \(\dfrac{d}{dx}\) applied twice to \(y\), and — as Remark 2.5 noted — the small \(2\)’s only count how many times we differentiate; they are not exponents. So \(d^{2}y\) means “\(d\) applied to \(y\) twice,” not \(d\) times \(2y\); and the \(dx^{2}\) is part of the symbol for differentiating twice with respect to \(x\) — not \(d(x^{2})\), where the \(x\) itself would be squared, and not an algebraic denominator to cancel or split.
為何此改正確：保留原塊的兩個防誤讀目標（非 \(d(x^{2})\)、非 \(d\cdot2y\），再加「不可約分」呼應 2.2-1），但不再宣稱 \(dx^{2}=(dx)^{2}\)；改採「整個符號＝operator 套兩次」的讀法，與 §2.2 caution、Remark 2.5 一致。Codex re-audit（餵入原 issue＋改寫）回 {"findings":[],"blockingCount":0,"verdict":"pass"}。
