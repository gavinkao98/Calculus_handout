Figure 6.4 signed-area-line essential §6.2 · Ex 6.5 之後 新

圖說（原文）：The integral \(\int_{0}^{2} (x - 1)\,dx\) as net signed area. The line crosses the axis at \(x = 1\); the triangle below the axis (marked \(-\), area \(\tfrac{1}{2}\)) cancels the congruent triangle above it (marked \(+\), area \(\tfrac{1}{2}\)), so the integral is \(0\) even though the total area enclosed is \(1\).

應尊重的定義域事實：過軸於 \(x=1\)；\([0,1]\) 軸下（\(-\)、面積 \(\tfrac12\)）、\([1,2]\) 軸上（\(+\)、\(\tfrac12\)）；+/− 靠 label＋色調、不只靠顏色（D8）；net \(0\) vs total \(1\) 只在 caption。

Figure 6.5 minmax-sqrt strong §6.2 · Ex 6.6 之後 新

圖說（原文）：Bounding \(\int_{1}^{4} \sqrt{x}\,dx\) without evaluating it (Example 6.6). On \([1, 4]\) the integrand runs from its minimum \(m = 1\) to its maximum \(M = 2\); the inscribed rectangle (area \(1 \cdot 3 = 3\)) and the circumscribed rectangle (area \(2 \cdot 3 = 6\)) trap the integral, so \(3 \le \int_{1}^{4} \sqrt{x}\,dx \le 6\).

應尊重的定義域事實：\(\sqrt{x}\) 遞增、上凹；inscribed 高 1（實線邊）、circumscribed 高 2（虛線邊）；只在端點碰曲線 \((1,1),(4,2)\)；面積 \(3,6\) 只在 caption。

Figure 6.6 accumulation-sliver essential §6.3 · 「accumulation function」子節 新（FTC-1 錨）

圖說（原文）：The accumulation function \(g(x) = \int_{a}^{x} f(t)\,dt\) is the shaded area up to \(x\). Advancing \(x\) by a small \(h\) adds the thin strip over \([x, x + h]\), of width \(h\) and height about \(f(x)\), so \(g\) grows by roughly \(f(x)\,h\) — its rate of change is the current height \(f(x)\). The strip is widened for visibility.

應尊重的定義域事實：\(f>0\)；陰影 \([a,x]=g(x)\)；薄片 \([x,x+h]\) 高 \(\approx f(x)\)；\(h\) 誇大、標「not to scale」；軸標 \(a,x,x+h\)；無 \(y\) 數值（label economy）。

Figure 6.7 ftc-trap strong §6.3 · FTC-1 proof 的 trapping 步 新

圖說（原文）：The trapping step in the proof of Part 1. Over \([x, x + h]\) the added area \(\int_{x}^{x+h} f\) lies between the inscribed rectangle of height \(m_{h} = \min f\) and the circumscribed rectangle of height \(M_{h} = \max f\), attained at \(u_{h}, v_{h}\); dividing by \(h\) brackets the difference quotient, and as \(h \to 0\) both tend to \(f(x)\).

應尊重的定義域事實：inscribed \(m_h=\min f\)（實線、較低）、circumscribed \(M_h=\max f\)（虛線、較高）；\(u_h\)（min、左）與 \(v_h\)（max、右）標出；callout \(m_h,M_h\to f(x)\)；\(h>0,f>0\)。小字級下標已回原始碼核對（無 \(m\leftrightarrow M\) 對調）。
