SC1 · det (warn) SC-adv derivative_of_sine — 導數定義行被丟 theorem_proof · #14

coverage.py：[SC1] derivative_of_sine.def（warn-only，exit 0）

  • SC1掉的步驟：定義行 \(\dfrac{d}{dx}\sin x=\lim_{h\to0}\dfrac{\sin(x+h)-\sin x}{h}\)。storyboard covers: [reduced, limits, qed] 刻意不含 def；proof.0 直接是 reduced form \(\dfrac{d}{dx}\sin x=\lim_{h\to0}\cos(x+\tfrac h2)\dfrac{\sin(h/2)}{h/2}\)。cite：storyboard derivative_of_sine.proof.0（yml:402）／contract required_steps.def（md）。
  • SC-adv初學者代價：LHS 一步從 \(\frac{d}{dx}\sin x\) 跳到 reduced 右式，未先立「導數＝差分商極限」這座橋；而該化簡在 difference_quotient_for_sine（#3）完成、距此約 11 場、中間無畫面 recap。旁白雖說「Start from the definition, and use the form we already found」，但畫面上 \(\cos(x+\tfrac h2)\dfrac{\sin(h/2)}{h/2}\) 對初學者像憑空冒出。對照 handout Theorem 3.1 Proof（sec-3-1.html:182–185）保留兩行對齊：先定義行、再 reduced。
  • 建議修法（propose，未施作）：把 def 納入 covers: 並讓 proof.0 顯示定義行、reduced 順延為 proof.1（limits→proof.2、qed 不變），與 handout 兩行對齊一致；或在 proof.0 前補一格「回顧：這條 reduced form 來自 §差分商」的畫面 recap。屬已鎖內容變更，需使用者裁決。
  • SC-honesty clean：reduced（標 recap_required）在 proof.0 確有在地重畫整條 \(\cos(x+\tfrac h2)\frac{\sin(h/2)}{h/2}\)（非只口頭指涉）→ SC2 在地覆蓋誠實。

SC-adv · adv derivative_of_cosine — limits 折進 qed theorem_proof · #15

VERDICT：0 PD, 0 OF, 0 SC blocking, 1 advisory

all_six_trig_derivatives derivation · #20
